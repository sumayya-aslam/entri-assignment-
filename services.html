Hotel accommodation

When planning a tour and booking hotel accommodations, it's essential to consider various factors beyond just the price and location. Here's a breakdown of details to look for in tour hotel accommodation: 1. Room Types and Amenities: Room Types: Different hotels offer various room types, such as single, double, triple, deluxe, or suites. Choose the type that suits your travel companions and needs. Amenities: Consider amenities like free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, parking, housekeeping, and even specific features like a balcony, bathtub, or preferred view. Additional Services: Some hotels offer additional services like breakfast in bed, birthday decorations, or special meals, enhancing your stay. 2. Hotel Features and Facilities: Fitness Facilities: If you enjoy staying active, check if the hotel has a fitness center, swimming pool, or other recreational facilities. Transportation: Consider the availability of transportation options, especially for tours and activities within the city. Check-in/Check-out Times: Confirm the check-in and check-out times to ensure a smooth arrival and departure. Hotel Location: Choose a hotel that is conveniently located near the attractions and activities on your tour itinerary. 3. Types of Accommodation: Hotels: A wide variety of hotels, from budget-friendly to luxury, cater to diverse needs. Bed & Breakfasts (B&Bs): B&Bs offer a more personal and cozy experience, often including breakfast in the morning. Resorts: Resorts offer a more comprehensive vacation experience with a variety of amenities and activities. 4. Booking and Payment: Booking Systems: Many online booking platforms manage reservations for various types of accommodations, including hotels, resorts, and vacation rentals. Payment Options: Confirm the accepted payment methods and any associated fees

Air Ticketing

To book tour air tickets, you can utilize online platforms like IRCTC Air, Goibibo, or Skyscanner, or contact travel agencies like Ashok Travels & Tours or local agencies in Kerala. These options allow you to search for flights, compare prices, and make bookings online or through a physical agent. Online Platforms: IRCTC Air: This platform, run by Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation, allows you to search for domestic and international flights, and book your tickets online, according to the National Portal of India. Goibibo: This platform offers a user-friendly interface for booking flights, comparing prices, and accessing various discounts and offers. Skyscanner: This search engine allows you to compare flight prices from multiple airlines and booking providers, helping you find the best deals. MakeMyTrip: A popular online travel agency that allows you to book flights, compare prices, and access various discounts and offers.
